About Me

My research focuses on creating precision quantum metrology platforms that both probe fundamental physics and pave the way for future industrial technology. Cavity optomechanics, quantum optics, and electronics are heavily utilized in my experiments, while on the theory side, quantum physics, stochastic processes, and other topics are extensively considered.
